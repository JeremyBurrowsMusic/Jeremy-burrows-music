Jeremy Burrows is a Newcastle-based bassist, arranger, and band leader with deep roots in jazz, funk, soul and latin music. Whether anchoring a tight rhythm section with electric bass or bringing warmth and resonance on double bass, Jeremy brings musicality and feel to every performance — and as an arranger and band leader, the vision and craft to shape an entire ensemble around it.

Based in Newcastle, NSW, Jeremy leads the soul-funk band Soul Junction NC and performs with a range of groups across the Hunter region — from intimate jazz trios to festival-ready ensembles.

Available for gigs, session work, and private events.

Soul Junction NC

Soul · Funk · Jazz · Bossa Nova

Soul Funk Jazz Bossa Nova

Soul Junction NC is a Newcastle-based band bringing infectious grooves and sun-drenched melodies to venues across the Hunter region. Drawing from the rich traditions of soul, funk, jazz, and bossa nova, the band delivers a sophisticated yet accessible sound that moves the room every time.

Led by bassist Jeremy Burrows, Soul Junction NC blends the warmth of classic soul with high-energy funk rhythms, the sophistication of jazz harmony, and the breezy feel of bossa nova — creating a sound that's equally at home in a relaxed bar setting as it is on a festival stage.

The band scales to suit your venue, budget, and requirements — from an intimate duo right up to a seven-piece ensemble.
